The holiday destinations in Korea, Australia, Italy and the United States of America are private experiences that are unique and designed exclusively for Shell customers.

Have a Soul to Seoul Experience
Week 1 – 4: Holiday in Korea – Jeju Island & Seoul City

A self drive exploration of Jeju Island’s striking scenery and beautiful islands, cliffs and beaches. After an informative safety briefing and introduction, immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of the island, explore breathtaking lava caves, trek up rocky peaks and be fascinated by Asia’s one and only seaside waterfalls. A quick flight will take you back to mainland where your experience will be combined with a private driven tour of ancient palaces with ample time for shopping and gastronomy pursuits in the heart of Seoul. Experience the top tier of accommodations with the famous 5* Jeju Lotte World and Fraser Place in Seoul.

Australia: The Southern Star Adventure
Week 5 – 8: Holiday in Southern Australia – Melbourne & Tasmania

A self drive exploration through Southern Australia’s charming fine wine region of Yarra Valley or the coastal cities along the scenic Great Ocean Road, intriguing towns and the world renowned Cradle Mountain National Park. Experience close encounters with species of the wilderness of Australia, abundant outdoor activities, spectacular landscapes of Tasmania’s mountains and historic town, and some of the finest hotels in the region, combined to make this an unforgettable journey. Together with a private evening tour of the vibrant city of Melbourne with drinks and dinner at a selected restaurant, experience the best Southern Australia has to offer.

The Great Italian Escape
Week 9 – 12: Holiday in Italy – Amalfi Coast

A unique discovery of Italy’s stunning Amalfi Coast. This self drive itinerary brings you through the rich history and beauty you will find along romantic Amalfi Coast – one of the world’s most thrilling and scenic roads veering close to the edge of cliffs around the jagged mountains. This achingly picturesque experience is further enhanced by the appreciation of the beauty of the Amalfi coastal towns like Sorrento and Positano. Be amazed by the UNESCO World Heritage Sites of Pompeii, a city frozen in time and buried under ash for fifteen centuries after the eruption of Mt. Vesuvius and revel in delightful Mediterranean cuisines, excursions to the luxurious Capri Island and the exploration of old and new Italian cities while staying in the beautiful coastal hotel set in an ancient convent.

'Viva Las Vegas'
GRAND PRIZE: Holiday in United States of America – Las Vegas & Grand Canyon

A once-in-a-lifetime adventure through the most exhilarating the West Coast has to offer. From Las Vegas, embark on an exciting drive to the Grand Canyon, National Parks, visiting towns and suburbs for a glimpse of life different from how you know it. Take in the magnificent Grand Canyon from a spectacular helicopter journey; visit and snap stunning photographs of the colourful and surreal Corkscrew Slot Canyons in a driven tour and wrap up the experience with glitz and glamour in Las Vegas, staying in the famous Venetian Hotel, with tickets to the famed Broadway classic – The Phantom of the Opera.
